Detailed Service Pages

In-Depth Look at Every Treatment We Offer

Explore each service in detail so you know exactly what to expect before your visit.

Teeth Cleaning in Hamilton

Professional dental cleaning, also called prophylaxis, removes plaque and tartar that brushing alone cannot reach. Our hygienist uses ultrasonic scalers and hand instruments to clean above and below the gumline, followed by polishing and fluoride application. Regular cleanings every six months prevent gingivitis, reduce bad breath, and help us detect cavities early. Patients with gum disease may need deeper scaling called root planing, which we perform comfortably with local anaesthetic.

Clear Aligners Hamilton

Clear aligners are a modern alternative to traditional braces, using a series of custom-made transparent trays to gradually shift teeth into alignment. We use advanced 3D scanning to map your treatment plan and show you the expected result before you begin. Aligners are removable for eating and cleaning, making oral hygiene easier than with fixed braces. Most cases take between six and eighteen months, with check-ups every six to eight weeks to monitor progress.

Dental Fillings Hamilton

When decay damages a tooth, a dental filling restores its shape, strength, and function. We use tooth-coloured composite resin that bonds directly to the tooth structure, avoiding the need to remove healthy tooth material. The procedure is quick, usually completed in a single visit with local anaesthetic for comfort. Modern composites match your natural shade so closely that the restoration is virtually invisible, even on front teeth.

Dentures Hamilton Ontario

Dentures replace missing teeth and restore the ability to chew, speak, and smile with confidence. We offer complete dentures for patients missing all teeth in an arch, and partial dentures that clip onto remaining natural teeth. Each denture is custom-crafted from impressions of your mouth to ensure a secure, comfortable fit. Modern dentures look remarkably natural and can be stabilized further with dental implants for patients who want added security.

Tooth Extractions Hamilton

Tooth extraction is sometimes necessary when a tooth is too damaged, decayed, or crowded to be saved. We perform both simple extractions for visible teeth and surgical extractions for impacted wisdom teeth. The procedure is done under local anaesthetic, and we provide detailed aftercare instructions to ensure smooth healing. For patients who want to replace the extracted tooth, we discuss implant, bridge, or denture options during the same visit.

Dental Implants Hamilton

Dental implants are the gold standard for replacing missing teeth. A small titanium post is surgically placed into the jawbone, where it fuses with the bone over several months in a process called osseointegration. Once healed, a custom crown is attached, giving you a tooth that looks, feels, and functions like a natural one. Implants preserve jawbone density, protect neighbouring teeth, and can last a lifetime with proper care.

Dental Braces Hamilton

Dental braces remain one of the most effective ways to correct complex bite issues, crowding, and misalignment. We offer traditional metal braces, ceramic tooth-coloured braces, and self-ligating systems that reduce friction and discomfort. Treatment typically lasts twelve to twenty-four months, with adjustments every four to six weeks. Braces are suitable for children, teens, and adults, and we coordinate with orthodontic specialists when advanced cases require it.

Root Canal Therapy Hamilton

Root canal therapy saves a tooth that would otherwise need extraction due to deep decay or infection. The procedure removes the infected pulp from inside the tooth, disinfects the canals, and seals them with a biocompatible material. Despite its reputation, modern root canal treatment is no more uncomfortable than a routine filling thanks to effective local anaesthetic. After healing, a crown is placed to protect the tooth and restore full chewing function.
